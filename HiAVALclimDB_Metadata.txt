Peak	Name of peak that was cimbed/attempted
Elevation	Elevation of peak
Country	Country peak is located in
Total_events	Number of individual avalanche events
Total_fatalities	Total number of fatalities
Staff_fatalities	Total number of fatalities of hired staff.
